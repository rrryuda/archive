42dot maps SDK

Overview

The 42dot Maps SDK has been developed by 42dot as a standard map guideline for providing map-based services. Its goal is to enhance key content tailored to different environments and purposes, and it is designed to facilitate integration with various services.

I improved the usability of the existing map and defined a standardized style for the entire map. Depending on the purpose of our in-house service, which provides route and navigation information, we carefully planned how information is presented on the map to ensure clarity.

Cartography

The POI(Point of Interest) icons are fundamental visual identity elements of the map and are detailed in our brand guidelines. They efficiently convey terrain and location information with each user interaction with the map, and all POI icons are designed to maintain a consistent representation.

Sprite image

The color system used for the map symbols is designed with the most perceptually distinct and functional colors, enabling users to accurately distinguish POI attributes. These colors are applied in a way that doesn't interfere with other prominent map content, facilitating better integration with various mobility services through our planning.

The key element of the map style, polygons, provides topographical information that goes beyond simple location data on the map. We have defined the prioritization of displaying information such as residential areas and educational facility complexes based on usage frequency. Our goal is to offer a user-centered map UX by providing a map experience that revolves around the users.

Navigation UI

In addition to maps, I have defined all visual elements used in the navigation GUI. These icons play a crucial role in route guidance, ensuring quick and clear recognition of essential information. Furthermore, the neutral visual style of these icons maximizes UI usability and reinforces brand identity.

To provide users with a consistent design experience throughout the vehicle infotainment system, I collaborated closely with the navigation team. As a result of this collaboration, the connected apps within the infotainment system exhibit distinct specialized usability while maintaining strong interconnectedness.

Map editor

UMOS Map Studio is a map authoring tool that allows creators to generate and edit map styles, icons, fonts, and prioritize POIs. It has been designed to provide an intuitive way for creators to visualize the hierarchy and specifications of all information, enabling them to create and adjust the map's style effectively.

UMOS map studio

All of 42dot's services actively leverage the creation of standard maps, as they are all map-based. Collaborating with engineers as needed, I established POI hierarchies tailored to the characteristics of each service and created advanced maps.
